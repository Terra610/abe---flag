How uploads, privacy, and receipts work

A.B.E. runs on a simple rule: your data stays on your device unless you choose to share it.

  • CSV uploads are processed locally in your browser using JavaScript.
  • No logins, no accounts, no hidden API calls to a backend.
  • You can download every result as CSV (and, on some pages, as JSON audit files).
  • If you publish anything, you decide what to redact before you send it to anyone.
If you can‚Äôt safely expose a name or case number, replace it with an internal ID. The math doesn‚Äôt care what the label is ‚Äî it only cares about the structure of the CSV.
