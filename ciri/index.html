⚙️ CIRI — Constitutional Integrity ROI Engine

Overview

The Constitutional Integrity ROI Engine (CIRI) calculates the financial recovery value generated when unlawful or unconstitutional enforcement actions are prevented or corrected. It models the monetary benefit of lawful compliance — translating every restored constitutional right into a measurable economic gain.

Core Formula

Impact_Value = (Legal_Score × ROI_Coefficient) + (Equity_Score × Social_Weight)
