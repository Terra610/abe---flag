System Identity

Define the constitutional constant as the lawful equilibrium signal:

$$ C_0 \;=\; \frac{F \cdot L \cdot M}{E_e} $$
  • \(F \in [0,1]\): constitutional fidelity  ¬∑  \(L\): liberty magnitude  ¬∑  \(M\): market integrity
  • \(E_e > 0\): economic entropy (corruption/inefficiency)

CDI ‚Äî Constitutional Divergence Index (signal)

Normalize observables to \([0,1]\) and combine with weights \(w_i\):

$$ \delta_t \;=\; \sigma\!\left(\beta_0 + \sum_i w_i\,g(z_{t,i})\right), \quad g(x)=2x-1,\ \ \sigma(x)=\frac{1}{1+e^{-x}} $$

SIRF ‚Äî Shouse Integrity Recovery Function (potential)

Convert divergence into recoverable potential \(P_t\) with capacity \(\kappa\), then pull \(F\) toward 1 at rate \(\alpha\):

$$ P_t=\kappa\,\delta_t \qquad\qquad F_{t+}^{(S)} \;=\; F_t + \alpha\,P_t\,(1-F_t) $$

IRE ‚Äî Integrity Restoration Equation (action)

Verified corrective action mass \(A_t\) (from CIRI total recovery) raises \(F\) and reduces \(E_e\) with saturation:

$$ \Delta F_t^{(I)}=\gamma\,\frac{A_t}{A_t+K_F} \qquad E_{e,t+1}=E_{e,t}\left(1-\psi\,\frac{A_t}{A_t+K_E}\right) $$

Combine with SIRF and small leakage \(\lambda\):

$$ F_{t+1}=\min\!\bigl\{1,\;F_{t}^{(S)}+\Delta F_t^{(I)}-\lambda(1-F_t)\bigr\} $$

CEQ ‚Äî Civic Efficiency Quotient (efficiency)

Map fidelity and entropy to system efficiency \(\eta_t \in [0,1]\):

$$ \eta_t \;=\; \frac{F_{t+1}^{\,\theta}}{1+\dfrac{E_{e,t+1}}{E_0}} $$

SIY ‚Äî Shouse Integrity Yield (human-scale return)

Translate efficiency into per-capita (or per-household) yield:

$$ Y_t \;=\; \frac{\eta_t\,P_{\max}}{N} $$

Constitutional Constant (updated)

$$ C_{0,t+1}\;=\;\frac{F_{t+1}\cdot L\cdot M}{E_{e,t+1}} $$

Parameter Starters

\(\alpha=0.25,\ \kappa=0.6,\ \gamma=0.3,\ K_F\approx K_E \approx\) 1‚Äì3√ó median \(A_t\); \(\psi=0.4,\ \lambda=0.02,\ \theta=1.3,\ E_0=\) baseline \(E_e\).

Implementation Sketch

P_t = kappa * delta_t
F_S = F + alpha * P_t * (1 - F)
dF_I = gamma * (A_t / (A_t + K_F))
F1  = min(1.0, F_S + dF_I - lambda_ * (1 - F))

eta = (F1 ** theta) / (1 + (E1 / E0))
Y   = (eta * P_max) / N
C0  = (F1 * L * M) / E1
