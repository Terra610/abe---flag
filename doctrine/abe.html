A Decent Proposal (A.B.E.)

What it is: A constitutional–economic repair engine that converts unlawful systemic drift into measurable recovery, without new taxes or outside capital. A.B.E. comprises three linked modules:

  • CIRI — measures constitutional deviation and quantifies recoverable value.
  • CIBS — allocates verified recovery into workforce, housing, youth, veterans, legal aid, etc.
  • CII — verifies benefits for employees, consumers, and communities.

Why it matters: It turns compliance loss into recurring savings and growth. In the automotive sector, full lawful alignment (zero drift) projects multi-billion savings and unlocks consumer access at scale.

CRRA — Citizen Rights Restoration Act (Enforcement)

What it does: Ends misapplication of federal commercial regimes to non-commercial citizens; restores lawful boundaries; and creates a recurring, debt-neutral recovery stream by redirecting misused funds to citizen outcomes.

  • Affirms constitutional supremacy; eliminates reliance on extra-textual doctrines that block redress.
  • Establishes audit/attestation and transparent pipelines from recovery → reinvestment.
  • Pairs legal alignment with measurable economic uplift (jobs, GDP, credit restoration).

Rebuild Together Plan — Allocations & Outcomes

What’s funded: $2.5–$3.0T two-year redeployment (no new taxes), with recurring annual streams from recovered misuse. Priority lines include mobility grants, vehicles, small-business capital, housing rehab, legal defense, veterans support, youth programs, digital access, and community safety infrastructure.

  • Jobs: 15–18M; GDP: $1.46–$1.58T uplift; Revenue: +$150B state/local.
  • Households: 1.18M housed; Mobility: 60M regain legal access to economy.
  • Justice: major reductions in incarceration and rights-denial costs.
