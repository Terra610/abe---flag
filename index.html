Mission

A.B.E. restores constitutional fidelity and economic integrity by aligning state practice with federal law, dismantling unlawful enforcement incentives, and reinvesting savings into people (housing, youth, veterans, farmers, digital access).

  • Protect life, liberty, and due process.
  • Stop misuse of federal transportation & safety funds.
  • Replace perverse incentives with transparent, measurable outcomes.

What A.B.E. Is

An interoperable blueprint: legal arguments + data models + implementation rails. Open-licensed (CC BY 4.0) so partners can deploy quickly and verify everything.
