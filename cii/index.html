Citizen Impact Index (CII)

Tracking real-world equity impacts through lawful resource reallocation.

The Citizen Impact Index (CII) measures how constitutional and economic reforms—like ABE and CRRA— translate into measurable quality-of-life gains across housing, healthcare, education, and justice access.

Data from the CII helps validate recovery pool allocations and supports transparent policy realignment under the American Butterfly Effect framework.
