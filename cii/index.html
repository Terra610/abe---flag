Citizen Impact Index (CII)

Tracking real-world equity impacts through lawful resource reallocation.

The Citizen Impact Index (CII) measures how constitutional and economic reforms—such as ABE and CRRA— translate into measurable quality-of-life gains across housing, healthcare, education, and justice access.

Each index point represents a verified impact in citizen prosperity. CII data validates Recovery Pool allocations and ensures transparency within the American Butterfly Effect framework.
